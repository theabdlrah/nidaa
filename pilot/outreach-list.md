# Nidaa — Initial Partner Outreach List (Execution Day 1)

Goal: 20–30 candidate organizations, tiered by reachability + strategic fit.
These are PUBLIC-FACING contacts (partnerships/info inboxes). VERIFY the current
address and the right person on each org's site before sending. Outreach is the
human's job — this list is the starting pipeline, not the send.

> Ranking principle (from strategy): choose the FIRST pilot for evidence-generation
> ability, not ultimate prestige. Diaspora/mutual-aid + mapping + student groups are
> reachable NOW and produce the referral that later unlocks large NGOs and Gaza orgs.

## Tier 1 — Diaspora / Gaza-solidarity mutual aid (highest reach + fit; start here)
1. The Sameer Project — thesameerplanet.org / thesameerproject.org — Gaza mutual aid, online, Arabic/English. Contact: site contact form / info@.
2. Gaza Soup Kitchen — gazasoupkitchen.org — grassroots Gaza aid coordination. Contact: site form.
3. HEAL Palestine — healpalestine.org — diaspora-led children/family aid. Contact: partnerships inbox on site.
4. Anera — anera.org — established diaspora humanitarian org (Palestine). Contact: info@anera.org.
5. Medical Aid for Palestinians (MAP) — map.org.uk — UK-based, verification-minded. Contact: info@map.org.uk.
6. Palestine Children's Relief Fund (PCRF) — pcrf.net — medical/humanitarian. Contact: info@pcrf.net.
7. Palestinian Red Crescent Society — prcs.org — recognized verifier-class actor. Contact: site form.
8. Palestinian Youth Movement — pyMovement.org — diaspora organizing network. Contact: site chapters.
9. National Students for Justice in Palestine — sjpnational.org — campus networks. Contact: site form.
10. Local city diaspora committees (e.g. your own city's Palestine solidarity group) — search locally; highest trust, lowest reach cost.

## Outreach sequencing (do NOT start at the bottom of prestige)
- Week 1–2: contact #1–#10 and #11–#13 first (reachable, fit, technical validators).
- Keep #4–#7 (large NGOs) as a SECOND wave once pilot-1 evidence exists.
- #8–#10 and #19–#26 are your fast "yes" fallbacks if Tier 1 is slow.

## What to send (first touch, <5 min to read)
The one-page Partner Brief + Do-No-Harm summary + a 90s demo link. Answer the
director's 5 pre-conversation needs: who you are, what it is/isn't, the ask, the
risk/safety story, and a peer reference (after pilot 1).
