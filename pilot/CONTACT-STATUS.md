# Contact Status

Live status board for active and pending outreach contacts. Updated as replies
and referrals arrive. State vocabulary matches OUTREACH-LEDGER.md five-state
machine (Queued / Contacted / Replied / Interviewed / Closed); columns below
track the finer-grained evidence pipeline.

Legend:
- **Contacted** — outreach sent, no reply yet.
- **Replied** — they responded; not yet a full conversation.
- **Evidence received** — concrete signal logged (quote/story/fact) usable in the tracker.
- **Follow-up required** — we owe them something (questionnaire, reply, intro) or they owe us a response.
- **Referral received** — they named another contact/org we should approach.
