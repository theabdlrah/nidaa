# Independent Observer — Recruitment Message (R1, feasible-or-document)

You do NOT need an expert. You need someone who can honestly answer:
  "Did this event happen?" and "Was it captured in the shared doc?"
A trusted friend, fellow student, or group member who is NOT the steward works.

------------------------------------------------------------------
DRAFT MESSAGE (adapt to channel; keep it honest, not a pitch)
------------------------------------------------------------------
"Hey [name] — I'm running a small coordination observation in [group]. I'm the
steward keeping a shared doc of who's doing what. I need a neutral second pair of
eyes: for the next ~3 weeks, would you occasionally glance at [group's channels]
and note down coordination events you see (a request, an offer, a decision) in a
simple log, plus whether I captured each in the doc? No decisions, no extra work
for the group — just watching and noting. Takes a few min every couple days. Want
to help? I'll send the one-line log format."

------------------------------------------------------------------
IF THEY AGREE
------------------------------------------------------------------
- Send them the observer column concept from COORDINATION-EVENT-LOG.md.
- They fill "Observed-by / Observer: entered?" — the steward does NOT grade himself.
- This breaks the circular self-measurement (C2 from the red team).

------------------------------------------------------------------
IF NO ONE IS AVAILABLE
------------------------------------------------------------------
Do NOT block the pilot. Instead, explicitly downgrade in all outputs:
  "Single-observer, founder-collected data. Results are EXPLORATORY."
That is weaker than independent observation but honest and still worth running.
