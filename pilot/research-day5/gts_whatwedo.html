Ground Truth Solutions was founded in 2012 to shift the prevailing narrative on humanitarian action, flipping the spotlight from aid agencies to people affected by crises around the world.  

Since then, GTS has worked on more than 120 projects in more than 35 countries, speaking with more than 100,000 people experiencing crisis worldwide. 

The belief that recipients of aid should have more of a say in how aid is provided inspired the development of the methodologies and values that drive the core of our work. Our insight is used to reimagine what equitable support systems can look like.  

Our goal is to make the perceptions and the priorities of crisis-affected individuals worldwide the touchstone and driver of systems that recognise their full agency and respond to their views, needs, and priorities.
