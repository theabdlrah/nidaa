OCHA (cluster-system architect / coordinator)

  narrative than a ground-level case.
- FIT: SECOND. Good to corroborate; less direct on the absorption discriminator.

CCCM (Camp Coordination & Camp Management cluster)
- Operational / field, but domain-narrowed to camp settings.
- Useful only where the case is camp-specific; the Nidaa reference context
  (Gaza Health Cluster referral chain) points at Health, not camps.
- FIT: THIRD. Case-specific relevance only.

COMMUNITY COORDINATOR
- Observes informal networks from the community / bottom side.
- Can describe informal persistence well, but is poorly positioned to
  authoritatively characterize the FORMAL system's absorption behavior — exactly
  the half OQ-2 hinges on.
- FIT: FOURTH. Strong on informal side, weak on the formal-system behavior that
  resolves OQ-2.
