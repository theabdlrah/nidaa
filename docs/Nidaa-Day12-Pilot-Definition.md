==================================================================
A. PILOT OBJECTIVE (single, falsifiable sentence)
==================================================================
Determine, in one real but non-humanitarian fragmented-coordination group, whether a
single designated steward using a low-burden capture tool can (i) keep structured
coordination information maintained and consulted under a real pressure event
[tests canonical A6b], and (ii) produce participant value that exceeds the steward's
maintenance cost [tests A8].

The pilot is built to be LOST: every pre-registered outcome below either strengthens,
weakens, or falsifies A6b and/or A8. There is no "inconclusive by design" exit.

==================================================================
B. HYPOTHESES UNDER TEST
==================================================================
H1 (canonical A6b / W-C):
  "Where a designated owner/mandate + low-burden capture exists, grassroots/informal
   coordinators maintain structured operational information during active coordination
   and under pressure (Reality A)."
  Tested directly. Prediction: maintenance holds + artifact is consulted, including
  through the pressure event.

H2 (A8, new):
  "The value generated by steward-maintained structured information exceeds the cost
   of maintaining it."
  Tested directly. Prediction: aggregate participant value (time saved + matches acted
  on) > steward time invested, sustained past the novelty window.

CONTROLLED / NOT TESTED HERE (named to prevent drift):
- W-A (informal networks emerge when formal overloaded): NOT tested. Proxy env has no
  formal coordination system to overload; this stays OPEN.
- OQ-1 (protracted transfer): NOT tested. Proxy is benign/short; cannot stand in for
  Gaza/Syria multi-year stress.
- OQ-2 (formal-fails vs coexist): NOT tested.
- G1/G2 humanitarian cases: NOT generated (no humanitarian access).

==================================================================
C. PILOT DESIGN
==================================================================
Design type: WITHIN-SUBJECT A/B (single group, two phases). No second group needed,
so it fits a single founder + limited resources while still giving a causal read:
baseline (no steward) shows natural fragmentation; intervention (steward) shows whether
structure changes it. This is the smallest design that can falsify (it includes the
absence condition Reality B as Phase 1).

Environment (PROXY — selection criteria, NOT a specific org pre-chosen):
  Required:
   - A real group (not simulated) of 6–12 people with genuine coordination needs.
   - Information genuinely fragmented across >=3 channels (e.g. WhatsApp + email +
     verbal/FB/Slack), with observable duplication, dropped requests, or "who's doing
     what" confusion.
   - A real, foreseeable pressure/stress event (deadline, surge of requests, one-shot
     deliverable) during the window.
   - Founder can be a trusted steward the group accepts.
   - NOT humanitarian, NOT conflict, NOT a formal NGO cluster (constraint: no
     humanitarian access). Benign fragmentation only.
  Candidate environments the founder may pick from (any ONE):
   1. A local volunteer/community event crew (cleanup, garden, mutual-aid-adjacent
      non-conflict group) coordinating via scattered chat.
   2. A hobby/student club whose comms are split across WhatsApp/email/FB.
   3. An online community coordinating real-world meetups.
   4. A small open-source project's contributor coordination (issues scattered).
  Pick the ONE the founder can access fastest with real participants.

Phases & duration:
  Total: 21 days.
  Phase 1 — BASELINE (no steward), days 1–7:
    Group coordinates normally. Founder observes + logs fragmentation signals only.
    No artifact, no steward role. This IS the Reality B reference.
  Phase 2 — STEWARD ACTIVE, days 8–21 (14 days, long enough to see past novelty):
    Founder acts as steward (see D). Structured artifact live. A planned or natural
    PRESSURE EVENT is scheduled for ~days 14–16 to test under-load maintenance.
  Why 14 days for Phase 2: Day 11 risk "adoption may collapse after novelty" requires
  observation beyond the first-week honeymoon. A 7-day Phase 2 could not detect decay.

Participants:
  - 1 steward = founder (designated owner/mandate satisfied by role assignment).
  - N = 8–12 target real participants; HARD MINIMUM 6 (below 6 there is no meaningful
    fragmentation or matching to observe). Recruited from the chosen environment.
  - No payment, no NGO, no humanitarian framing. Informed mildly that founder is
    "tracking coordination so things don't fall through cracks" (truthful, not a study
    pitch).

Minimum workflow to be tested (5 actions, no product, one existing tool):
  W1 CAPTURE: each observed coordination event (request / offer / decision / open gap)
      recorded by steward as ONE row in a single shared structured doc (Google Sheet /
      Notion / markdown). Target SLA: within 24h, <=2 min per row (low-burden).
  W2 SURFACE: steward posts a 1–3 line digest at fixed cadence (every 2–3 days) into
      the group's existing channel. Visibility only.
  W3 MATCH: participants consult the artifact to find needs/offers; steward nudges
      obvious matches. Outcome = a match acted upon.
  W4 PRESSURE: during days 14–16 a real/staged surge tests whether W1–W3 hold under
      load (does maintenance continue or collapse?).
  W5 CLOSEOUT: end-of-pilot participant check-in on whether the artifact changed
      anything for them (value signal).
  Tooling: ONE existing shared doc. No custom software. No features beyond these 5.

==================================================================
D. STEWARD DEFINITION
==================================================================
Role: the founder, explicitly assigned as the designated owner of the structured
      artifact (satisfies A6b's "owner/mandate" condition by design).
Mandate: capture coordination events, surface a digest, nudge matches. NOT to make
      decisions for the group, NOT to adjudicate, NOT to become the single point of
      failure by hoarding info (artifact is shared/read-only-visible to all).
Low-burden capture: <=2 min/row, one doc, no formatting tax, no approval workflow.
Time budget cap: steward logs ALL minutes; if it exceeds a pre-set weekly cap
      (e.g. 5 hrs/week) that itself becomes A8 cost evidence.
Failure mode to watch (steward-induced): if maintenance only happens because the
      founder is unusually diligent (not replicable), that is a WEAKEN signal for A6b
      generality, not a clean pass.

==================================================================
E. METRICS (success / what we count)
==================================================================
Capture fidelity (A6b):
  - Capture rate = coordination events entered / events observed (denominator from
    Phase 1+2 event log). Target for "holds": >=80%.
  - Collapse gap = longest consecutive days with >=1 expected event but zero captures.
    Target: no gap >2 days in Phase 2, including through pressure event.
Consultation (A6b):
  - % participants who report consulting the artifact at least weekly (survey).
    Target: >=50%.
  - Passive signal if tooling allows: view/edit counts on the doc (optional).
Continuity under pressure (A6b):
  - During days 14–16, capture rate stays >= baseline Phase-2 rate (no drop >20%).
Value (A8):
  - Steward cost = total logged steward minutes (capture+digest+matching+fixing).
  - Participant value = (sum of self-reported minutes saved) + (count of matches acted
    upon). Convert to common unit (minutes) for ratio.
  - Target for "value>cost": value >= 2x cost, sustained in week 2 of Phase 2.
  - OBJECTIVE OUTCOME MEASURE (R4, added post-red-team): dropped-request /
    unresolved-gap rate and duplicate-work count, measured Phase 1 (baseline) vs
    Phase 2. This anchors A8 value in OBSERVED coordination outcome, not only
    participant perception. A8 "pass" with rising perceived value but flat/no
    improvement in objective outcome = weak; the objective measure is the
    higher-tier evidence. Also serves as the real test of "did structure change
    anything" (guards against the Day-11 risk that info improvement moves no
    outcome).
Novelty decay (Day 11 risk):
  - Compare week-1 vs week-2 Phase-2 consultation + capture rates. A drop >30% = decay
    signal (feeds A6b weaken / A8 weaken).

==================================================================
F. FAILURE CONDITIONS (pre-registered falsification)
==================================================================
A6b (canonical) is FALSIFIED if, with steward + low-burden capture PRESENT:
  - Capture rate <50%, OR
  - A collapse gap >3 days occurs (especially during/after pressure event), OR
  - <25% of participants consult the artifact by end of Phase 2 (adoption collapse),
    OR
  - Maintenance drops >20% during the pressure event and does not recover within 3 days.
  (Any one triggers falsification of the conditional claim — conditions met yet it
  failed.)

A6b is WEAKENED (not falsified) if:
  - Capture 50–80%, OR minor gaps (<=2 days), OR consultation 25–50%, OR maintenance
    holds only because founder was unusually diligent (not generalizable).

A6b is STRENGTHENED if:
  - Capture >=80%, zero gaps >2 days, >=50% consult weekly, maintenance holds through
    pressure event.

A8 is FALSIFIED if:
  - Value < Cost (steward minutes > participant benefit), OR
  - Visibility does NOT translate to outcomes (artifact consulted / matches listed but
    nobody acts; "info improved, nothing changed"), OR
  - Value collapses after novelty (week-2 value < week-1 by >50%), OR
  - The real bottleneck was NOT information (participants cite trust/authority/
    resources, not fragmentation) — info improvement produced no outcome movement.

A8 is WEAKENED if:
  - Value 1–2x cost, OR decays toward parity after novelty, OR benefits accrue only to
    a subset.

A8 is STRENGTHENED if:
  - Value >=2x cost AND sustained past novelty AND outcomes (matches acted on) occurred.

==================================================================
G. EVIDENCE COLLECTION PLAN (PR-1 ENTER / PR-2 PRESERVE)
==================================================================
All raw; no summary-as-evidence. Source-of-truth hierarchy honored
(raw > intake > log > ledger).

1. STRUCTURED ARTIFACT (primary raw data): the shared doc itself, snapshotted at end of
   each Phase-2 week + immediately after pressure event. Versioned copies kept.
2. COORDINATION-EVENT LOG: every request/offer/decision observed across ALL channels,
   timestamped, with a yes/no "entered artifact?" flag. This is the capture-rate
   denominator. Founder logs during both phases (Phase 1 = baseline fragmentation).
3. STEWARD TIME LOG: daily minutes per category (capture / digest / matching / fixing).
   The A8 cost numerator.
4. PARTICIPANT CHECK-INS: 2 short surveys (mid Phase 2 ~day 14, end ~day 21). Questions:
   did you consult the artifact? did it save you time (estimate min)? did you act on a
   match? what still fell through? Open text. Verbatim preserved.
5. OBSERVATION NOTES: founder's weekly 15-min reflection — adoption signals, confusion,
   observed duplication, whether the group kept using chat instead of the artifact.
6. PRESSURE-EVENT RECORD: what happened, volume, did W1–W3 hold, founder's minute spend
   that day.
Per PR-2: participant replies and event log are RAW; surveys saved verbatim; the
artifact snapshot is raw. No assumption may cite a summary when these exist.

==================================================================
H. DECISION RULES (what each result does to A6b / A8)
==================================================================
A6b (canonical):
  strengthen -> capture >=80%, no gap >2d, >=50% consult, holds through pressure.
  weaken     -> capture 50–80% / minor gaps / 25–50% consult / holds only via unusual
                founder diligence.
  falsify    -> capture <50% OR gap >3d OR <25% consult OR >20% maintenance drop at
                pressure unrecoverable. (Conditions present, still failed.)

A8:
  strengthen -> value >=2x cost, sustained past novelty, matches acted on.
  weaken     -> value 1–2x cost, or decays toward parity, or benefits subset-only.
  falsify    -> value < cost, OR visibility≠outcomes (consulted but no action), OR
                value collapses after novelty, OR real bottleneck was not information.

Cross-read (the thesis-level signal Day 11 warned about):
  A6b holds BUT A8 falsified -> "info CAN be maintained, but it isn't worth the cost."
    This is the single most decision-relevant Day-11-risk outcome; it kills the thesis
    without touching A6b. Flag it first if it appears.
  A6b falsified -> even the maintainability premise fails; thesis dead at the root.
  Both strengthen -> proceed to a harder (still proxy) replication before any context
    claim; do NOT leap to humanitarian generalization (OQ-1/OQ-2 remain open).

==================================================================
PRE-COMMIT GATE (R3, added post-red-team)
==================================================================
Before day 1: RECORD the chosen proxy group + a one-line rationale stating WHY that
group has a real, observable coordination problem (not "it's convenient"). This blocks
selection bias — the temptation (intentional or not) to pick a friendly group that
makes the experiment look better, or a cold one that dooms it. The group pick is
locked at day 1; changing it mid-pilot invalidates the result. Logged in
proxy-pilot/EXECUTION-KIT.md.

==================================================================
INDEPENDENT OBSERVER (R1, added post-red-team — feasible-or-document)
==================================================================
Preferred: recruit ONE independent observer (a trusted third party watching the
group's channels) who logs coordination events AND whether the steward captured each.
This breaks the circular self-measurement (the steward must not be the sole judge of
his own capture rate).
If no observer can be recruited: DO NOT block the pilot. Instead explicitly downgrade
evidential weight in all outputs:
  "Single-observer, founder-collected data. Results are EXPLORATORY."
That is weaker than independent observation, but it is honest and still generates a
result worth having. The downgrade is stated up front, not discovered later.

==================================================================
INTERPRETIVE GUARDRAIL (R5, added post-red-team — MANDATORY reading of any result)
==================================================================
A proxy result — strengthen OR falsify — does NOT resolve or falsify the CANONICAL
A6b (per A6b Canonical v1.0 §5–§6, which require a first-hand coordinator story from
the actual connectivity-stressed / protracted-crisis population, falsified only by a
real coordinator who HAD owner/mandate yet still collapsed).

Correct readings ONLY:
  "The proposed mechanism STRENGTHENED in this proxy environment."  OR
  "The proposed mechanism WEAKENED in this proxy environment."
Never:
  "A6b is true."  /  "A6b is false."  /  "A6b validated."  /  "A6b falsified."
Misreading a proxy falsify as "A6b falsified" is a governance failure, not a
conclusion. The proxy is a mechanism probe; it feeds the canonical resolution only
alongside real-population evidence from the practitioner pipeline.

==================================================================
I. RECOMMENDATION (updated post-red-team — READY WITH REQUIRED DESIGN CHANGES)
==================================================================
RUN IT, after applying R3 (pre-commit group) + R4 (objective outcome metric) + R5
(interpretive guardrail) + R1 (observer if feasible, else explicit downgrade).
This is the smallest design that can actually falsify both targets:
- Within-subject A/B needs no second group → fits single founder + limited resources.
- 21 days with a 14-day intervention catches novelty decay (a named Day 11 risk).
- Pre-registered falsification thresholds remove post-hoc wiggle (constitution-aligned).
- One existing doc, 5 workflow actions, zero product.

SECOND PROXY (R2, post-red-team — CONDITIONAL, not mandatory):
  A second proxy (different group + ideally a non-founder steward) is the recommended
  replication to separate the founder effect from the mechanism. It is REQUIRED only
  if the first proxy is ambiguous or shows strong founder effects. If the first proxy
  is strong, well-documented, and an NGO is willing to collaborate on a LIMITED
  OBSERVATIONAL field trial, it is reasonable to proceed to Phase 2 while clearly
  acknowledging limitations. A Phase-2 NGO field trial is ITSELF an experiment, not a
  declaration that the mechanism is validated.

Do NOT, under any reading of a "strengthen" result, claim it validates the Nidaa
humanitarian thesis. The proxy cannot speak to OQ-1/OQ-2. Apply the R5 guardrail
above to every result statement.

If A8 falsifies while A6b holds, stop building and write the negative result up.
That is a successful pilot, not a failure.

==================================================================
LIMITATIONS (carried with every result — non-negotiable honesty)
==================================================================
- Proxy environment only. Mechanics of maintenance/cost in a benign group != crisis
  coordination. OQ-1 (acute→protracted) and OQ-2 (formal-fails vs coexist) stay OPEN.
- Single founder = single steward; "owner/mandate" is satisfied by self-assignment,
  not by group election. Generalizability of the owner condition is untested.
- N is small (6–12); this is a falsification probe, not a statistical demonstration.
- No humanitarian access → G1/G2 real cases NOT produced here. Those still require
  the practitioner pipeline already in the ledger (Mones/Hisem/Aaron/Emma).
- "Value" relies partly on self-reported minutes; treat as ordinal, not precise. The
  R4 objective outcome measure (dropped-request rate) is the higher-tier anchor.
- If no independent observer was recruited (R1), results are EXPLORATORY, single-
  observer, founder-collected — explicitly downgraded, not silently weakened.
- Per R5, a proxy result does NOT resolve/falsify canonical A6b. It strengthens or
  weakens the proposed mechanism IN THIS PROXY ENVIRONMENT only.
